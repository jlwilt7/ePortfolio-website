My dad is also a Network Engineer for Community Aid so he instilled his love of computers and technology within me. College

When searching for a college to continue my education, I decided that I wanted to go to a Christian school. I grew up Mormon and eventually became atheist then Christian. The decision to search for Christian schools if possible came pretty late. I eventually applied to Messiah University, and got in, thankfully. It had my major, a good program, it was close to home, and it was Christian.

Now I am a sophomore here at Messiah, and I love it. I decided to pick up a Web Development minor last year and continue my interest past what I learned in my 9th Grade class. I am motivated to learn more as a hobbiest but I am thinking of potentially designing websites for churches that have old looking designs that need revamped.

Major Experiences

Getting My First Job at LEAF

I got my first job in my freshman year at a "farm". Though I use the word farm loosely as it was more of a large scale garden. My coworkers and I were youth interns and we were responsible for tending to the plants on the farm and harvesting them. This experience was big for me as it showed me how to both talk to people from different settings and farm plants. It also taught me the value of money and working for my own things.

Joining Cross Country & Track

In my junior year of high school I decided to join the cross country team after much deliberation. COVID left me simultaneously starving for social interaction and afraid of it, haha. But I joined and made a lot of friends there and broke many records in 5k races.

Leaving the Mormon Church

In my junior year I also decided to leave my parents church, which was a big shocker to them, naturally. I just did not really believe in God anymore and needed to have some room to breathe from their religion. Over the next year or so, I became more agnostic in my thinking until I started to attend my friend's Non-Denominational church as I wanted to find meaning in life.

Getting the Job at Sheetz

In my senior year I had finally gotten my license and applied to Sheetz. I interviewed for the position and got the job and have been working there since then.

Joining the Orthodox Church

In March 2023 I decided to attend an Orthodox Church for the first time, and fell in love with the faith. I had been feeling empty and confused in my protestant tradition and needed a change. I was catechized a few months after my first attendance and then spent the next year learning. I was baptized in April of 2024.

Going to Messiah University

2023 was my first year at Messiah, and while it was a rough start trying to keep my head screwed on with all of my school work, I eventually got the hang of it. I made a good friend group, got good grades, and learned a lot. I am still enjoying my major and my time here.
